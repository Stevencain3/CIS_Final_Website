How to Submit Your Custom Design

  1. Choose Your Image: Select high-quality images (JPEG, PNG, TIFF). Maximum size 10MB.
  2. Adjust Your Image: Resize to fit our product dimensions
    • Shirts: 12"x16"
    • Tumblers: 8"x3"
  3. Upload and Preview: Use our tool to position your image on the product.
  4. Submit and Confirm: Add to cart, checkout, and approve via email confirmation.
  5. Approval and Production: Production takes 5-7 business days post-approval.
